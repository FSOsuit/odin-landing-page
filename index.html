About Bunnies

Bunnies eat more than 30 times a day. They love fruits and different veggies. Just be careful to not overfeed your rabbit and stick to basics.

In the wild bunnies like to dig holes, at home they should get their own roof over their head.

Bunnies love to cuddle and sleep next to you, just not for the long time.

Curiosity is one of their best characteristics and they will explore every corner.
